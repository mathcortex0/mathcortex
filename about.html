Our Story

Born on 17 March 2026

Alamin Network was founded on 17 March 2026 with a single conviction — that the world deserves journalism that is honest, precise, and fearless. Headquartered in Dhaka, Bangladesh, we launched with a commitment to cover the stories that matter most, from the corridors of power in Dhaka to the far reaches of the global stage.

From day one, Alamin Network was built differently. As a fully digital, independent news organization, we are not bound by the constraints that have long shaped traditional media. We answer only to our readers and to the truth.

Our name carries a meaning and a promise. Every report we publish — whether breaking news, in-depth analysis, or sports coverage — is held to the highest standard of factual accuracy and editorial integrity.

Our Vision

Dhaka Today. Everywhere Tomorrow.

We began in Dhaka, but our ambition has no borders. Alamin Network is building toward a future where our correspondents and bureaus are present in every corner of the world — from South Asia to the Americas, from Africa to Europe.

We believe that the world is better informed when quality journalism reaches every community, regardless of geography. Our digital-first infrastructure means we can scale to any region without compromising on the editorial values that define us.

In every city we enter, we will do the same thing we do in Dhaka — report accurately, challenge power, and give voice to those who are rarely heard.

Mission, Values & Commitment

🎯
Our Mission
To deliver accurate, independent, and timely journalism that empowers citizens to understand the world around them and hold power to account.
⚖️
Editorial Independence
Our editorial decisions are made by journalists alone. No advertiser, government, or external interest influences what we report or how we report it.
🌍
Global Ambition
Starting from Dhaka, we are building a network with the scope and reach to report from every region of the world with the same rigour and independence.
Editorial Standards

What We Stand For

✅
Accuracy First
Every fact is verified before publication. We correct errors promptly and transparently when they occur.
🔍
Accountability Journalism
We believe journalism's highest purpose is to scrutinize power — political, corporate, and institutional.
🤝
Fairness & Balance
We present all credible perspectives and give every subject the opportunity to respond before we publish.
🛡️
Source Protection
We protect our sources with absolute commitment. No external pressure will ever compel us to reveal a confidential source.
⚡
Speed Without Compromise
We move fast to break news, but never at the cost of accuracy. Being second with the truth beats being first with a mistake.
🌐
Public Interest
Every story we choose to tell is evaluated against one question — does this serve the public's right to be informed?
